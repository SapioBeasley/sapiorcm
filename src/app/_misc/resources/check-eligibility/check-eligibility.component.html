**This is to be done before the patient is seen by the doctor!

Along with the customers demographic details you will also need, insurance group number (npi), and the policy number. With the exception of the patient's name (if they are not the policy holder) this information is located on the insurance card.

After the insurance provider has been contacted and all requirements have been met, the billing process should be explained to the patient. The patient should be informed of services not covered by their insurance plan and the out-of-pocket costs of those services. Having all parties understand and agree to their financial responsibility ensures the medical billing process will proceed smoothly.
